Understand Investments. Protect Your Future.

Our mission is to empower individuals to make informed personal finance decisions by providing clear, accessible insights into common investment options such as stocks, mutual funds, savings accounts, certificates of deposit, and the S&P 500. We also highlight inflation’s impact, so you can make confident, long-term choices.

Instructions

Start with $10,000. Each year you can move money into different accounts (e.g., Savings). Use Next Year to advance up to 5 years. The Investment Hub is where you buy; the Sell Assets page is where you withdraw/sell. Year and Cash are shown in the top bar on those pages.
